# Dataset Templates

This document defines the structured templates used to generate the historical QA dataset.

---

## A. Historical Knowledge Understanding

### I. Dialog-based

1. `[p1]` and `[p2]` met at a restaurant over tea and discussed `[p3]`. Is this possible? *(True/False)*

   * `[p1]`: Person 1
   * `[p2]`: Person 2
   * `[p3]`: Historical topic

2. During a university seminar, Prof. X argued that `[p1]` while Prof. Y countered with `[p2]`. Which perspective aligns with historical evidence? *(MCQ)*

   * `[p1]`: Historical claim
   * `[p2]`: Alternative view

---

### II. Narrative-based

1. In year `[p1]`, `[p2]` occurred. What happened next? *(MCQ)*
2. If `[p1]` had not `[p2]`, what would likely have been different? *(MCQ)*
3. Arrange these events: `[p1], [p2], [p3], [p4]` *(MCQ)*
4. During `[p1]` in `[p2]`, which country was allied with `[p3]`? *(MCQ)*
5. In `[p1]`, `[p2]` could `[p3]`. Verify the claim. *(True/False)*
6. Population estimate for `[p1]` around `[p2]` *(MCQ)*
7. Which technology did NOT exist during `[p1]` *(MCQ)*

---

### III. Document-based

1. A letter dated `[p1]` mentions `[p2]`. *(True/False)*
2. A `[p1]` newspaper headline reads `[p2]`. *(True/False)*
3. A diary from `[p1]` claims `[p2]`. *(True/False)*
4. Newspaper headline `[p3]` in `[p1]` — is it accurate? *(True/False)*
5. Archaeological vs written sources conflict: `[p1]` vs `[p2]` *(MCQ)*

---

## B. Cause–Effect

### I. Event-centric

1. `[p1]` led to `[p2]` *(True/False)*
2. `[p1]` occurred before `[p2]` *(True/False)*
3. Were `[p1]` and `[p2]` simultaneous? *(True/False)*
4. Events between `[p1]` and `[p2]` leading to `[p3]` *(MCQ)*
5. Which event resulted from `[p1]` *(MCQ)*
6. Which crisis resembles `[p1]` in outcome *(MCQ)*

---

### II. Figure-centric

1. `[p1]` is credited with `[p2]` *(True/False)*
2. `[p1]` and `[p2]` were contemporaries *(True/False)*
3. Compare contributions of `[p1]` and `[p2]` *(MCQ)*
4. Who was `[p1]` of `[p2]` in `[p3]` *(MCQ)*
5. How `[p1]` influenced `[p2]` *(MCQ)*
6. Modern issue aligned with `[p1]` *(MCQ)*
7. Leaders similar to `[p1]` *(MCQ)*

---

### III. Fill in the blanks

1. The `[p1]` of `[p2]` was caused by ____ *(MCQ)*
2. Treaty signed by `[p1]`, `[p2]`, and ____ *(MCQ)*
3. Missing link: `[p1], ___, [p2]` *(MCQ)*

---

1. What was the primary catalyst? *(MCQ)*

3. Most plausible explanation for `[p2]` *(MCQ)*
4. Intended outcome of `[p1]` *(MCQ)*
5. Impact of `[p1]` on `[p2]` *(MCQ)*
6. Short-term effects *(MCQ)*
7. Long-term effects *(MCQ)*
8. What changed most between `[p1]` and `[p2]` *(MCQ)*
9. Shift in research focus *(MCQ)*
10. Impact analysis *(MCQ)*
11. Defense of actions *(MCQ)*
12. Activity participation *(MCQ)*
